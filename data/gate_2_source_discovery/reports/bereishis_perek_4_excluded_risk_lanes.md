# Bereishis Perek 4 excluded risk lanes

This report names lanes intentionally excluded from the review-only safe candidate inventory. Exclusion here does not mean the material is invalid; it means this task does not have enough reviewed support to treat it as safe for Perek 4 packet planning.

| Lane | Why excluded now | Future review required |
|---|---|---|
| Translation/context | Contextual meaning can become a translation task rather than noun recognition. | Teacher review plus explicit translation/context lane criteria. |
| Suffix/compound morphology | Perek 4 includes nearby forms such as צֹאנ֖וֹ, מִנְחָתֽוֹ, and מִנְחָת֖וֹ that should not be widened into simple suffix lanes. | Teacher and engineering review with validator support. |
| Advanced verbs | Many Perek 4 verbs are not part of this basic noun-recognition discovery lane. | Explicit verb-form lane support, distractor rules, and tests. |
| Vav hahipuch | Not supported by this source-discovery task. | Separate standards/skill review and validation. |
| Rashi/commentary | No commentary source/provenance layer is used here. | Separate Rashi/commentary source policy and teacher review. |
| Higher-order comprehension | Would assess interpretation rather than source-backed word-type recognition. | Teacher-authored assessment goals and rubric. |
| Ambiguous particles/direct-object markers | Low instructional value and easy pattern-guessing risk for this lane. | Teacher review and explicit function-word lane if ever used. |
| Unclear source/provenance items | Perek 4:17-4:26 do not yet have Batch 005 source-to-skill discovery rows. | Source-to-skill discovery/provenance layer first. |

All excluded lanes remain non-runtime, non-reviewed-bank, non-protected-preview, and non-student-facing.
