# Perek 3 Pilot Teacher Decision Checklist - 2026-04-29

Purpose: help Yossi or a teacher review the unresolved pilot issues before implementation. This checklist records decisions later; it does not apply decisions now.

## Decision options

- approve fix direction
- revise wording
- source follow-up needed
- suppress item until reviewed
- reject item/question type
- observe again

## Items for review

- Evidence: Student 1 was not sure what `What form is shown?` means.

- Decision needed: Is the proposed direction of replacing vague `form` wording with clearer student-facing verb-form language approved?
