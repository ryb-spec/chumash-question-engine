# Perek 3 Short Re-Pilot Scope - 2026-04-29

## Purpose

This scope defines a narrow short Perek 3 re-pilot after Yossi's language decisions. It tests only the remediated Perek 3 areas and explicitly excludes unresolved language/source lanes.

This scope does not activate Perek 4, does not widen runtime scope, does not promote content, and does not create fake data.

## Test in short re-pilot

- Revised tense/form wording.
- Revised prefix prompt wording.
- `דֶּרֶךְ` translation distractor repair.
- `אֲרוּרָה` translation distractor repair.

## Exclude from short re-pilot

- `אָשִׁית` / `שית` shoresh item.
- Unverified phrase_translation items.
- Perek 4 content.
- Any runtime expansion.

## Pilot size and mode

- Students: 1-2 students.
- Questions per student: 8-10.
- Required mode: Learn Mode only.
- Teacher monitor or pilot monitor should be open.

## What to watch for

- Do students understand the revised tense/form wording?
- Do students understand the revised prefix wording?
- Do the repaired `דֶּרֶךְ` distractors feel fair and meaning-level?
- Do the repaired `אֲרוּרָה` distractors feel fair and meaning-level?
- Does any question still feel like a distractor-elimination trick?
- Does any question accidentally test context instead of the intended word/skill?

## Pass criteria

- Students can understand the revised wording without teacher rephrasing.
- `דֶּרֶךְ` and `אֲרוּרָה` no longer produce obvious distractor-quality concern.
- Teacher does not flag a new wording or distractor issue in the tested lane.
- No excluded `אָשִׁית` / `שית` or unverified phrase_translation item is treated as closure evidence.

## Fail or revise criteria

- A student remains confused by the revised wording.
- A repaired distractor set is still flagged as unfair or misleading.
- A tested question appears to require context/translation beyond the intended skill.
- Excluded items accidentally become part of the evidence claim.

## Safety boundary confirmation

- No runtime scope expansion.
- No Perek 4 activation.
- No reviewed-bank/runtime promotion.
- No full Perek 3 closure claim.
- No source-truth change.
- No phrase_translation logic change.
