# Perek 1 Round 1 Pipeline Audit

This audit summarizes the completed Bereishis Perek 1 Round 1 path from verified source coverage through the internal protected-preview packet milestone. It is documentation only. It does not generate content, approve reviewed-bank use, approve runtime use, or approve student-facing use.

## Reusable pipeline stages

Round 2 should reuse the same safety structure in four compressed gates: readiness, input/template controls, controlled draft packet, and internal protected-preview packet. The reusable pieces are artifact naming conventions, TSV schemas, batch-balance reports, README metadata, Yossi review packets, validator/test pairing, and closed downstream gates.

## Manual review that should not be automated away

Yossi review remains required for extraction exceptions, enrichment verification, eligibility decisions, row-level pre-generation decisions, controlled draft review, and internal protected-preview packet review. Codex may prepare packets and apply explicit decisions, but should not infer approvals.

## Round 1 compression lesson

Round 1 built the rails. Round 2 can move faster by combining already-approved family policies and templates, but it must stop on unverified evidence, high-risk rows, verb-form rows without policy, Hebrew corruption, missing balance reports, or any accidentally opened protected-preview release, reviewed-bank, runtime, or student-facing gate.
