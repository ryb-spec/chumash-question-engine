# Bereishis Perek 3 to Perek 4 launch gate

## Purpose

This launch gate defines what must be true before starting Perek 4 candidate discovery.

It does not start Perek 4 candidate discovery. It does not create Perek 4 candidates. It does not activate runtime. It does not promote reviewed-bank content. It does not create student-facing content.

## Perek 3 completion signals

- Perek 3 internal packet exists.
- Perek 3 review checklist exists.
- Perek 3 review decisions are applied.
- Perek 3 item 004 revision plan exists.
- Perek 3 three-item readiness lane exists.
- Perek 3 blocked register exists.
- Perek 3 handoff exists.
- Perek 3 observation intake exists.
- Validators/tests pass.

## Perek 3 unresolved items

- Actual observations not yet recorded.
- Observation decisions not yet applied.
- Item 004 remains blocked.
- No runtime promotion.
- No reviewed-bank promotion.

## Go/no-go recommendation

- Go for Perek 4 source-discovery only.
- No for Perek 4 runtime activation.
- No for Perek 4 reviewed-bank promotion.
- No for Perek 4 student-facing content.
- No for broad Perek 4+5 expansion together.

## Perek 4 starting scope recommendation

- Start with Perek 4 only, not Perek 4 and 5 together.
- Start with source-to-skill discovery and candidate inventory only.
- Prioritize simple concrete nouns and clearly source-backed vocabulary.
- Avoid translation/context, suffix/compound morphology, advanced verbs, vav hahipuch, Rashi/commentary, and higher-order comprehension unless explicitly reviewed later.

## Required Perek 4 guardrails

- fail-closed
- no runtime
- no reviewed bank
- no student-facing content
- no fake expansion
- no broad candidate generation
- every candidate must preserve source/provenance
- duplicate-token/session-balance risk must be detected earlier than it was in Perek 3
- all Perek 4 outputs must be review-only until a later explicit task

## Perek 4 first-task recommendation

Create a Perek 4 source-to-skill discovery and safe-candidate inventory, not a packet. Identify possible basic_noun_recognition and vocabulary candidates. Include duplicate-token/session-balance warnings. Include source/provenance. Keep all gates closed.

## Safety boundary

No Perek 4 candidates are created by this launch gate. No runtime activation, reviewed-bank promotion, protected-preview packet creation, or student-facing content creation occurs here.
