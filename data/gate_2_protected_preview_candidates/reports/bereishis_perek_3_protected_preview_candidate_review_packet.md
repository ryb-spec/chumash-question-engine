## Decision Key

Use the same protected-preview candidate decision vocabulary as the Perek 2 process:

- `approve_for_internal_protected_preview_packet`: approve for a later internal protected-preview packet only, not runtime or reviewed bank.
- `approve_with_revision`: candidate may continue only after specified revision.
- `needs_follow_up`: more source, wording, or skill-lane review is needed.
- `reject_for_preview`: do not use this candidate for protected-preview review.
- `source_only`: preserve source mapping but do not use as a question candidate.

## Recommended Review Instructions

Review each row for whether the Hebrew token can safely support basic noun recognition without pulling in dialogue, theme, prefix/article, suffix, verb-form, or phrase-translation complexity. Any row with unresolved wording risk should remain `needs_follow_up` or `approve_with_revision`.
