# Bereishis Perek 1 First-Batch Distractor Planning Policy

This policy defines future distractor constraints only. It does not create distractors for individual rows.

## vocabulary_meaning

Future distractors must come from approved vocabulary pool only, be same general type where possible, avoid theological/sensitive distractors, avoid absurd choices, and avoid unreviewed meanings.

## basic_noun_recognition

Future distractors must distinguish simple categories only, avoid advanced grammar labels, avoid adjectives/verbs unless explicitly reviewed, and avoid confusing students with constructs/suffixes.

## direct_object_marker_recognition

Future distractors must avoid teaching false translations of `את`, must not say `את` means "the", must not say `את` means "with", and must test function carefully.

## shoresh_identification

Future distractors must use plausible but reviewed root choices only, avoid roots from unresolved or compound forms, and avoid binyan/stem distractors.
